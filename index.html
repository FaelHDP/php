Here are the frequently asked questions:

Artist

What rights do I get when buying a track?

The Buyer gets 100% of the royalty rights of the track in a definitive and irrevocable way, being able to explore it in every way and by any means. All rights and revenues generated by the track are exclusively yours.

What files do I get when buying a track?
  • The complete track (MP3 and WAV) in its mastered and non-mastered version.
    If the song contains vocals, you will also get the instrumental versions (with no vocals) mastered and non-mastered.
  • All individual elements of the track (STEMS) such as guitars, synths, piano, drums, etc. Including everything else you need to modify the track if you wish.
  • All MIDI files, used to create the elements of the track which contain melodies.
Can I monetize the track I just purchased?

Yes, the Buyer can release and monetize the track on all streaming platforms such as Spotify, YouTube, Apple Music. Beatport etc. And profit over marketing campaigns, videos, radios, among other means.

Is it possible to ask for refund or exchange of a track?

At first, no refund will be offered to the users regarding track purchasing, for the following reasons:

  1. Digital media: all services and compositions are produced in digital format. Thus, once acquired, its return becomes infeasible;
  2. Exclusivity: Each track is transferred only once to a single User. That way, its return would imply violation according to the exclusivity agreement settled between the Users.
  3. Previous evaluation: All tracks published on the site can be listened to previously and in its totality by the User that wants to purchase it. That way, the User knows exactly what composition is being purchased.

However, even though proper precautions are adopted, it is possible that certain situations demand refunds by Subclap, when, for example, a track has been plagiarized. In that case, we will take care of each matter in an individual way.

Is each track sold only once?

Yes, each track is sold only once. After being sold, it will automatically go out of stock and will no longer be available to the public.

How can I know if the vocal of the track is exclusive?

On the track page you can find all additional information, including the type of vocal used.

  • Royalty Free Vocals, means free to use for anyone.
  • Exclusive Vocals, means that the vocals have been recorded only for the specific track.
  • Instrumental, means that the track does not contain vocals.

Producer

How can I sell a track through Subclap?

The seller only needs to register on Subclap and submit a track for review through your dashboard.

How do I know my track was approved for sale?

Our team will analyze all files thoroughly within 1 to 3 working days and you will receive an email with the approval or the adjustment requirements.

Am I allowed to sell a product at any price?

The pre-established prices of a track vary from $199 to $499 dollars for tracks available at the sales catalog but the more you sell on Subclap, the higher your track prices can be.

How will I receive my earnings?

After a product is sold, the due amount will be transferred to the sellers dashboard. To withdraw the amount, the seller must only request the withdrawal.

How long does it take for the payment to be mande?

After the withdrawal request, the time for receiving the amount is up to 5 working days.

Am I allowed to delete the track from the platform at any times?

Sure, you can remove your track from Subclap at any time.

How much will I receive for selling a track?

All sellers will receive a percentage of 70% of every product sold by them. But just like the track prices, the more you sell on Subclap, the higher your earnings percentage can be.

Am I allowed to announce the same track on other platform?

No, it is strictly forbidden to announce the same track on different platforms. This can lead you to a permanent ban from both platforms. When announcing a track, the seller accepts the terms and conditions of use on the platform and gives in an exclusive license that will be active up to moment the track is sold or removed from the website.

If you decide to submit your track to another platform, just remove it from Subclap first.
